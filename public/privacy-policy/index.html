Privacy Policy

We ask for your age and electorate in order to serve you pages that are tailored to you. This information is kept on your device, and will not be sent to us or a third party.

The information that you submit in the mock election is anonymous, unidentifiable, and kept on a secure server. It will be deleted every year. We use Formester to manage the backend of the mock election page. Read their privacy policy.

We use EmbedQuiz for the quizzes in the modules. Read their privacy policy. We do not keep a record of your responses.

We use Easypolls for our polls on some of the pages. Read their privacy policy.
